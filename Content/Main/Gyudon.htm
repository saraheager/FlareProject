Gyudon (beef bowl) (牛丼)

 

 

Gyudon

牛丼

 

 

No one regrets a beef bowl.

Ingredients

For the Gyudon

  • 2 sirloin steaks, cut into strips

  • 125 ml dashi, on page 1see dashi, or dashi powder

  • 2 Tbsp mirin

  • 3 Tbsp sake

  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce

  • 1.5 Tbsp white miso

  • 1.5 tsp sugar

 

Toppings (optional)

  • 1 Tbsp shichimi togarashi

  • 1.5 Tbsp pickled ginger

  • Ramen egg, on page 1

  • 1 spring onion

Steps

  1. Combine dashi, mirin, sake, soy sauce and sugar in a wok, bring to a simmer over medium heat.

  2. Add the miso to a strainer and allow to dissolve in the dashi mixture.

  3. Add the steak and cook until done. The time will depend on how thin you slice the steak.

  4. Continue cooking to reduce the sauce to your liking.

  5. Serve over rice and top with a ramen egg, shichimi togarashi, pickled ginger and spring onions.
