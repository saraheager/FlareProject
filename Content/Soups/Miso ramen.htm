Miso ramen (味噌ラーメン)

 

 

Prep: 30 min

Cook: 1.5 hr

Serves: 3

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp sesame oil

  • 4 garlic cloves

  • 2 pieces of ginger

  • 1 onion

  • 3 spring onions

  • 2 Tbsp sesame seeds, toasted

  • 125 g ground pork, optional White pepper, to taste

  • 1 tsp gochujang

  • 3 Tbsp red miso

  • 1 Tbsp white miso

  • 1 Tbsp sugar

  • 2 Tbsp sake

  • 1.5 liters chicken stock, on page 1see chicken stockvegetable stock, on page 1see vegetable stock

  • 2 tsp soy sauce

  • White pepper, to taste

 

Toppings (optional)

  • Nori (海苔)

  • Ramen egg

  • Spring onions

  • Corn

  • Pak choi

  • Tofu (豆腐)

  • Chicken breast

 

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat sesame oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the garlic, ginger, onion and spring onion, and stir fry until fragrant.
  3. If using, add the pork and stir fry until cooked through.
  4. Add gochujang and red and white miso. Stir quickly to incorporate with the meat and to prevent burning.
  5. Add the sesame seeds, sugar and sake and cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Add chicken stockvegetable stock and bring to a gentle simmer.
  7. Add soy sauce and pepper to taste.
  8. Simmer for 1 hour.
  9. Serve over noodles, on page 1see ramen noodles, with your favourite toppings.
