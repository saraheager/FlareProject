Miso soup (味噌汁)

Miso soup is a traditional Japanese soup consisting of dashi stock and miso paste. Additional ingredients vary depending on regions and seasons.

Prep: 15 min

Cook: 20 min

Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 500 ml dashi

  • 100 g tofu

  • 2 Tbsp white miso

  • 1 Tbsp red miso

Steps

  1. Bring dashi to a simmer.

  2. Add red and white miso to a strainer and allow to dissolve in the stock.

  3. Add tofu and cook until heated through.

  4. Ladle into warmed bowls and serve immediately.
