Taiyaki (たいやき)

First sold in Japan in the early 1900s, taiyaki is a Japanese fish-shaped pancake commonly filled with red bean paste. Other common fillings include custard, chocolate or sweet potato.

Prep: 10 min

Cook: 25 min

Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 170 g butter, room temperature

  • 65 g brown sugar

  • 2 eggs

  • 200 g flour

  • 40 g corn flour

  • 2 tsp baking powder

  • 150 g soyamilk, warm

  • 8 g honey

  • 1 tsp soy sauce

  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine the milk, honey, soy sauce and vanilla extract.
  2. Cream the butter and sugar together until pale and fluffy.
  3. Add the eggs, one at a time.
  4. Add half the flour and cornflour followed by half the milk mixture. Repeat for the remainder.
  5. Heat a taiyaki pan over low heat and brush with oil or melted butter.
  6. Half fill the molds, and add your filling (I use Nutella).
  7. Pour more batter over the filling until it is completely covered.
  8. Close the lid, flip, and cook for 3 minutes. Flip again and cook for another 3 minutes.
  9. Cook until golden brown and crispy. Serve warm.
