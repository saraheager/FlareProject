Chicken stock (食パン)

Prep: 15 min

Cook: 6.5 hr

Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 2 kg chicken wings

  • 3.5 l water

  • 2 onions

  • 4 spring onions

  • 15 g ginger

  • 6 garlic cloves, crushed

  • 5 dried shiitake mushrooms

  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large pot or donabe, heat 1 Tbsp oil over medium-high heat.

  2. Add the ginger and garlic and stir fry until fragrant.

  3. Add the onions, spring onions, shiitake mushrooms and water.

  4. Bring to a boil, then simmer with the lid on for 6 hours.

  5. Strain the stock, season with salt and pepper to taste, and discard the solids.
